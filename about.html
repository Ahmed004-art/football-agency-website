About Us

Football Agency Sierra Leone is a premier sports management firm dedicated to discovering, developing, and promoting exceptional football talent from Sierra Leone to the global stage.

Founded in 2020, we've successfully placed over 50 players in professional clubs across Africa, Europe, and beyond. Our commitment to player welfare, professional development, and ethical representation sets us apart in the industry.

We believe every talented player deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, and we work tirelessly to make those dreams a reality.

Our Mission

To empower Sierra Leonean football talent by providing world-class representation, comprehensive career management, and access to international opportunities. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and player advocacy while contributing to the growth and recognition of Sierra Leone football on the global stage.

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from player development to client service, ensuring the highest standards in all our operations.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices, building trust with players, clubs, and partners.

Player-First

Our players' well-being, growth, and success are at the heart of every decision we make, ensuring they receive dedicated support throughout their careers.
